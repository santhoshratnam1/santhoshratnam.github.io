  • Role : Programmer/Level Design

    Estimation : 6 weeks

    Ragball(Unity)

    Ragball is a Free-For-ALl, isometric 3D arena sports game with ragdoll physics. Players Can Run, Dodge, and Throw using twin-stick movement and aiming. Players can also choose one of 4 characters here are 2 balls in play. Get hit with a ball and you’re sent into a ragdoll state Knocked Catch a ball thrown at you (a small timing window, unique to each character) to set yourself up for a shot. parry while holding another ball to send the thrown ball directly back at the other player. Each game is played on a 3-minute timer. One point is awarded for every successful hit with the ball. The players with the most points when the time runs out wins. This was a 4 man team project,Team Bonding in this project helped us to create this awesome game
